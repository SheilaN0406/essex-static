Schools and learning

How to apply for a school place during the normal admission rounds, and apply for a school place during the school year.

School admissions

You can apply for an Essex school place if you live in:

  • Basildon
  • Braintree
  • Brentwood
  • Castle Point
  • Chelmsford
  • Colchester
  • Epping Forest
  • Harlow
  • Maldon
  • Rochford
  • Tendring
  • Uttlesford

Southend-on-Sea and Thurrock are not in the Essex County Council administrative area.

You should read the admission booklets for:

  • secondary schools
  • primary schools

You may also want to check the priority admission catchment area schools.

Apply for a school place

For a secondary school place, you can apply online from September 12 2019 to 31 October 2019.

For a primary school place school place, you can apply online from 11 November 2019 to 15 January 2020.

Apply for mid-year admissions

You can apply to change your child's school during the school year.

For a primary school mid-year admission, you can apply online.

For a secondary school mid-year admission, use the mid-year secondary school application form (DOC, 52KB) and send it directly to the school.

Contact the school admissions team

Get in touch if you have a query about your application or if you are changing address.

Email: admissions@essex.gov.uk.
